Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Any data you share with us is used only to provide services and improve your experience, and it is never shared with third parties without your consent.

Who We Are

Information We Collect

  • Contact information (name, phone, email)
  • Service details (address, preferred times, device type, issue description)

How We Use Information

  • To schedule and deliver onsite services

Sharing & Disclosure

We may share limited information with service technicians, logistics partners, or payment processors solely to fulfill your request. We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Security & Retention

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

Your Choices
