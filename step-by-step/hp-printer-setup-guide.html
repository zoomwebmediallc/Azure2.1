Set up your HP printer with any device easily.

follow our step-by-step guide

1
Unpack and Prepare the Printer
  • Remove all packaging materials from the printer
  • Connect the power cord and turn on the printer
  • Install the toner cartridge as per the instructions provided
⚠️ Important

Ensure all protective tape and packaging materials are completely removed before powering on.

2
Load Paper
  • Open the paper input tray
  • Adjust the paper guides to fit the size of the paper
  • Place the paper into the tray and close it
3
Install the HP Smart App
  • Download and install the HP Smart app on your computer or mobile device
  • Ensure your device is connected to the same Wi-Fi network you intend to connect the printer to
📱 Download Links

Available on App Store, Google Play, and HP's official website for desktop versions.

4
Connect the Printer to Wi-Fi Using HP Smart
  • Open the HP Smart app
  • Tap the plus sign (+) to add a new printer
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to connect the printer to your Wi-Fi network
5
Alternative Connection Methods
🔒 Wi-Fi Protected Setup (WPS)
  • Press the WPS button on your router
  • Within two minutes, press and hold the Wireless button on the printer until the wireless light starts blinking
  • The printer will automatically connect to the network
🔌 Ethernet Connection
  • Connect an Ethernet cable from the printer to your router
  • The printer will automatically obtain an IP address and connect to the network
🔗 USB Connection
  • Connect a USB cable from the printer to your computer
  • Follow the prompts in the HP Smart app to complete the setup
6
Restore Network Settings (If Needed)
  • Press and hold the Information button for 3 seconds
  • Press and hold the Wireless and Cancel buttons simultaneously until the printer restarts
  • The wireless light will blink, indicating the printer is in setup mode
🔧 Troubleshooting

Use this method only if you're experiencing connection issues or need to reset network settings.

🎉 Setup Complete!
